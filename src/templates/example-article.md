## What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) is the practice of structuring your web content so that AI language models can accurately understand, cite, and recommend your business.

Unlike traditional SEO which targets search engine crawlers, GEO targets the language models that power **ChatGPT**, **Perplexity**, **Claude**, and **Gemini**.

## The Three Pillars of GEO

**1. Entity Clarity** — Make it unambiguous who you are, what you do, and where you operate. AI models need named entities (legal company name, location, founding year) to correctly attribute information to you.

**2. Fact Density** — Replace vague claims with verifiable numbers. "Many customers" becomes "2,400 customers across 14 countries since 2018". AI models cite specific, verifiable facts 3.7× more often.

**3. Schema.org Structured Data** — Embed machine-readable JSON-LD on every page. At minimum: `@type`, `name`, `url`, `description`, and `address`.

## Quick Start Checklist

- Add your legal entity name to every page's JSON-LD
- Replace all vague adjectives with specific numbers
- Generate an `llms.txt` file for your domain
- Run a GEO audit to score your entity clarity (1–10)
- Target a Fact Density score above 40
